The Solution

Three Guarantees

Every event that passes through our pipeline earns three independent proofs — not one database entry that can be quietly edited.

⚡

Real-Time Capture

Webhooks, not polling. Events are captured the instant they happen — zero temporal gaps, zero missed transactions. If it happened, we saw it.

🔒

Cryptographic Seal

Every event is hashed and written to a write-once evidence store at T+0ms. The original record can never be altered, deleted, or disputed.

₿

Bitcoin Inscription

Event hashes are batched into Merkle trees and inscribed as Ordinals on Bitcoin. Permanent, verifiable, anchored to the world's most secure ledger.
