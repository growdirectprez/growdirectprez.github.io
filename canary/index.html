Case Management
Fox Case Management
When an alert needs investigation, Fox organizes the evidence. Structured case workflows with full audit trails, linked transactions, and exportable documentation.

Immutable Evidence Locker

Every piece of evidence is INSERT-only at the database level — enforced by triggers, not convention. Each record is SHA-256 hashed on upload. Every access logged with actor, timestamp, and IP. No record can be modified or deleted once written, by any actor, including administrators.

Enforcement: 22 database triggers · SHA-256 hash chain

Structured Case Timeline

Append-only chronological record of every action taken on a case. Cannot be edited or deleted. From initial Chirp alert to final resolution, the complete investigative history is preserved exactly as it happened.

Chain of custody: Every transition logged

Alert-to-Case Escalation

When Chirp detects a cluster of related alerts — same employee, same pattern, escalating severity — Fox can automatically create a case pre-populated with all triggering transactions, detection rules, and evidence links.

Automated pre-population from Chirp alerts

Documented Resolution

Every case closure requires a structured resolution: verbal warning, written warning, suspension, termination, ban, police report filed, insurance claim, or no action. Closure requires a documented resolution note — no case can be silently closed.

8 resolution types · Mandatory documentation
