01 — The Problem
The Record Is Broken
Billions of events happen every day. Almost none of them are provably immutable.
⏱

Polling Gaps

Billions of events lost between polling intervals. The moment something happens and the moment it's recorded are never the same. That gap is where truth disappears.

🔒

Mutable Records

Every event stored in a traditional database can be altered, deleted, or lost. Admins can change history. Servers go down. There is no external proof anything happened as recorded.

🏦

Trust Required

The current system requires you to trust the institution that controls the record. The bank. The card network. The intermediary. No proof it happened. No proof it wasn't changed.

02 — The Solution
Capture. Seal. Inscribe.
Three steps. One result. A record no one can argue with.
1
REAL-TIME

Webhooks, Not Polling
